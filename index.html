Privacy Policy of 'SLASHI MUSIC'

This Privacy Policy describes how SLASHI MUSIC collects, uses, and discloses your information. It applies to your use of the App on Android and/or iOS devices

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following information to provide and improve the App:

Device Information: We collect non-personally identifiable information about your device, such as device type, operating system version, and a unique device identifier. This information is used for troubleshooting purposes and to improve the App's performance.

Media Access: With your permission, the App will access your device's music library to allow you to play your own music files. We do not collect or store any information about your music files themselves.

2. Use of Information

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

To operate and improve the App: We use device information to diagnose and fix problems in the App and to improve its overall performance.

To personalize your experience: We may use device information to personalize your experience within the App, such as remembering your recently played songs.

3. Sharing of Information

We do not share your information with any third-party companies or organizations.

4. Security

We take reasonable steps to secure your information from unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, or destruction. However, no internet or electronic storage system is 100% secure.

5. Your Choices

You can control the information collected by the App by:

Granting or denying storage and media access permission.

6. Changes to this Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on the App.

7. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at hashiqali3080@gmail.com.

8. Permissions Explained

Storage Permission: This permission allows the App to access your device's storage to play your music files.

Read Media Permission: This permission is deprecated in newer Android versions and is replaced by Storage Permission. It allows the App to access your music library.

9. Important:

This policy emphasizes that you are not collecting any personal data from the user.

It clarifies the purpose of device information and media access permissions.

It assures users that their music files are not collected or stored.
